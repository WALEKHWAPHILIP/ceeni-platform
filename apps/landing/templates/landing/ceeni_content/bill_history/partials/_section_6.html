6. Rationale for the EEPR Bill, 2025

The Ethnic Equity and Public Representation Bill, 2025 is designed to move Kenya from aspirational commitments to binding, measurable, and enforceable standards for inclusivity in public appointments and representation.

6.1 Translating Constitutional Ideals into Action

  • Establishes precise targets tied to national demographic data.
  • Mandates annual equity audits for every public body.
  • Creates a statutory oversight body — CEENI — with enforcement powers.

6.2 Ending Informal Bargaining and Patronage

  • Replaces political deals with a transparent algorithm.
  • Aligns public appointments to population proportions.
  • Eliminates ethnic dominance in key positions post-election.

6.3 Binding Oversight and Corrective Mechanisms

  • CEENI can nullify non-compliant appointments.
  • It issues legal compliance orders and recommends sanctions.

6.4 Transparent, Public-Facing Accountability

  • KPEID Dashboard publishes real-time staffing data.
  • Equity Scorecards track and grade public body performance.
  • Enables public monitoring and leadership accountability.

6.5 Safeguarding National Cohesion

  • Proactively addresses exclusion to prevent ethnic grievances.
  • Fosters national belonging and shared trust in state institutions.
  • Aligns with Article 10 values — unity, inclusivity, social justice.

6.6 Timeliness and Urgency

  • Deep-rooted exclusion will persist without legal reform.
  • The next administration must inherit a functioning framework.
  • Action before the next election avoids another cycle of division.
