The Historical Roots of the EEPR Bill

How Kenya’s history shaped the need for the Ethnic Equity and Public Representation Bill, 2025

Ethnic exclusion has deep roots in Kenya’s public life. From post-independence centralisation to post-election patronage, ethnic identity has long shaped access to jobs, power, and services. The EEPR Bill, 2025 turns those ideals into action — through clear targets, binding obligations, and public accountability. Understanding this history is the first step to building a fairer future.
