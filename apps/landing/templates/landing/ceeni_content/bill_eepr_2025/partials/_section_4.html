4. Part IV — Data and Reporting Obligations

Mandatory data submission.

(1) Every public institution shall, at the end of each financial year, submit to the Commission a report detailing the ethnic composition of its workforce, board membership, senior leadership, and any appointments made during the reporting period.

(2) The data submitted under subsection (1) shall be disaggregated by —

(a) ethnic community;

(b) gender;

(c) position and job group or rank; and

(d) terms of service (permanent, contractual, temporary, or internship).

Publication of reports.

(1) The Commission shall, within three months of receiving institutional data, compile, analyze, and publish a national Ethnic Equity Report covering —

(a) national trends and patterns of representation;

(b) performance of individual institutions against established benchmarks;

(c) cases of overrepresentation or underrepresentation;

(d) gaps, risks, and recommendations for compliance.

(2) The Commission shall make the report accessible to the public through —

(a) its official website and equity dashboard;

(b) printed copies available at its offices and Huduma Centres;

(c) direct submission to Parliament, county assemblies, and the Cabinet.

Real-time equity dashboard.

The Commission shall develop and maintain a public-facing digital dashboard that provides —

(a) live data visualization of ethnic representation across ministries, counties, parastatals, and agencies;

(b) downloadable reports and compliance summaries;

(c) updates on benchmarks, tribunal rulings, and equity audits.

Verification and audit.

(1) The Commission shall have power to verify the accuracy of any data submitted and may conduct independent audits where necessary.

(2) Any public officer who knowingly submits false or misleading data commits an offence and shall be liable, upon conviction, to a fine not exceeding five hundred thousand shillings or to imprisonment for a term not exceeding one year, or to both.

Ethnic Equity Register.

The Commission shall maintain an official Ethnic Equity Register containing annually updated data on the representation status of each public institution, accessible to the public.
