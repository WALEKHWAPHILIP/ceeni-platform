7. Closing Call

Kenya’s strength is in our people — every tribe, every language, every culture.

For too long, our differences have been used to divide us.

It’s time to turn our diversity into our greatest advantage.

The Ethnic Equity and Public Representation Bill, 2025 is our chance to make sure that public service truly belongs to all Kenyans.

This is about fairness.

This is about unity.

This is about building a country where every child can dream without limits.

Let’s make history — together.
Support the Bill. Stand for fairness. Strengthen Kenya.
