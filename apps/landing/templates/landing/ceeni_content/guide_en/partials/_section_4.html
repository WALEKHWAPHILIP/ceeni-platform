4. How It Works

The Bill makes fairness simple and transparent:

1. Know the Numbers

  • Kenya’s population by ethnic group will be confirmed using the latest official data.
  • These numbers will guide how many positions each group should fairly have.

2. Apply the Formula

  • When hiring or promoting staff, public bodies must use the proportional allocation formula in the Bill’s Fifth Schedule.
  • This ensures no group is over- or under-represented.

3. Report and Publish

  • Every public institution must submit staffing data to CEENI regularly.
  • CEENI will publish this data on the KPEID Dashboard, so anyone can check.

4. Check for Compliance

  • If a public body is not following the rules, CEENI can issue orders to correct the imbalance.
  • This may include cancelling unfair appointments and re-advertising jobs.

5. Protect Whistleblowers

  • People who report unfair practices will be protected by law.

This system removes guesswork, prevents political favouritism, and makes fairness the standard, not the exception.
