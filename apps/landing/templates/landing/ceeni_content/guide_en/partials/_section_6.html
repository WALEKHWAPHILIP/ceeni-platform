6. What You Can Do

The success of the Ethnic Equity and Public Representation Bill, 2025 depends on all of us. Here’s how you can help:

1. Learn and Share

  • Read the Bill in simple language (available on the CEENI app and website).
  • Share the message of fairness and unity with friends, family, and community groups.

2. Engage Your Leaders

  • Ask your MP, Senator, or MCA to support the Bill in Parliament.
  • Attend public forums and contribute your views during public participation.

3. Use the KPEID Dashboard

  • Check the staffing data for your county, ministry, or agency.
  • If you see unfairness, report it through the CEENI Citizen Reporting Portal.

4. Speak Up Safely

  • Use the whistleblower protections to report discrimination without fear.

5. Lead by Example

  • Promote inclusivity and fairness in your own workplace and community.

This Bill is more than a law — it’s a national agreement that public service belongs to all Kenyans equally.
