3. What the Bill Does

The Ethnic Equity and Public Representation Bill, 2025 turns the promise of fairness into action by:

1. Setting Clear Rules

  • Every public body must reflect Kenya’s ethnic diversity in its staffing.
  • Each community’s share of jobs must be proportional to its population size.

2. Creating a Watchdog

  • The Commission for Ethnic Equity and National Inclusion (CEENI) will monitor and enforce these rules.
  • CEENI will have the power to investigate, order corrections, and penalise non-compliance.

3. Making Data Public

  • A national KPEID Dashboard will show staffing numbers by ethnicity for all ministries, counties, and state agencies — open for all to see.

4. Enforcing Fairness

  • If an appointment violates the rules, CEENI can order it cancelled and the position re-advertised.

This means no more secret deals — just clear, fair, and transparent processes that everyone can follow.
