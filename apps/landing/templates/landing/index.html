Let’s Build a Kenya That Includes All of Us

Where Fairness Is the Rule - Not the Reward

CEENI (Commission for Ethnic Equity and National Inclusion) is Kenya’s bold, data-driven step away from tribal arithmetic and toward institutional fairness. This platform supports the Ethnic Equity and Public Representation Bill, 2025 (EEPR Bill, 2025) - a law designed to end ethnic exclusion, regulate tribal campaigning, and guarantee public jobs reflect Kenya’s full face: diverse, proportional, and just.
No more domination. No more tokenism. No more fear every five years.
Just truth in data, power in unity, and dignity in how we govern - for every Kenyan, in every county.

How It Will Work - The Institutions That Will Make Equity Real

From the grassroots to Parliament - these are the structures that will turn the Ethnic Equity Bill into action.

CEENI

Independent commission to monitor fairness, publish reports, and enforce compliance across public institutions.

KPEID Dashboard

A live, public dashboard showing how fairly ministries, counties, and agencies reflect Kenya’s diversity.

Equity Scorecard

Annual grading system for public institutions based on ethnic inclusion and hiring fairness.

CEENI Tribunal

Legal arm to hear complaints, enforce rulings, and hold institutions accountable under the new law.

Citizen Reporting Portal

Citizens can safely report bias, exclusion, or unfair hiring - anonymously and with protection.

Parliament & Cabinet

Parliament receives CEENI’s reports. Cabinet implements them. Together, they make equity actionable.
